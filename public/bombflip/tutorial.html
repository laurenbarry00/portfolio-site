The Game Board

The game board consists of a 5x5 grid of cards. Each card can either have a point value (1, 2, or 3) or a bomb. Click on a card to turn it over and reveal its value.


Beyond the 5x5 grid, there is one more column and row each. These give clues as to what values each row and column contain. See the section on Info Squares for more information about them.

Scoring, Winning, and Losing

Ways to Score

1 Card: +1 point

2 Card: Multiply score by 2.

3 Card: Multiply score by 3.

Winning and Losing

To win: reveal all the numbered cards on the board without hitting a bomb.

Hit a bomb? The board explodes and you lose!
